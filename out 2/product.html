Core Modules

Operator-focused tooling for managed liquidity programs.

Market Making

Automated bid/ask liquidity with full control over ladder shape, spread, randomness, and inventory bias.

Volume Bot (Fill-Based)

Execute target daily volume using real trades only, with configurable trade sizes and pacing.

Price Follow (Master / Slave)

Separate price discovery from execution. Follow external markets without placing orders on the master exchange.

Price Support (budgeted)

Maintain a price floor using a dedicated budget. Includes auto-stop and notifications when funds are depleted.

Manual Trading (role-gated)

Place manual limit or market orders directly from the UI. Protected by role permissions and re-authentication.

Production Operations

Dockerized deployment, health checks, runner heartbeat monitoring, and automated database backups.

Price Intelligence (CEX + DEX)

Combine centralized exchange prices with on-chain DEX price feeds. Use DEX data as a master reference, or as a safety validation layer.

  • Read-only DEX price feed (no DEX trading)
  • Ideal for low-cap and illiquid pairs
  • Detect CEX anomalies with cross-source validation

What it is / What it is not

DEX Price Feed is read-only. No on-chain trading.

AI Advisory (Human-in-the-loop)

uLiquid AI reviews market structure, order books, and execution stats to recommend better parameters.

  • Read-only recommendations
  • No autonomous trading
  • Use suggestions as presets

What it is / What it is not

AI provides recommendations only. No autonomous trading.
